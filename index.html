The everyday gap

Age ratings are broad. Individual scenes are not.

Parents cannot preview every episode, clip, trailer, or recommended video before a child presses play. WatchWise adds a calm safety layer during playback, helping reduce exposure to specific moments that may be too intense, mature, or distracting for your family.

It is not a spy tool or a screen-time monitor. It is designed around transparent content protection for video, with settings parents can see, tune, and protect.

How it works

Near-live protection without turning family viewing into a project.

01

Samples the scene

WatchWise checks selected frames and available subtitles while video is playing.

02

Classifies sensitive signals

Local AI and trusted segment data score categories like violence, language, nudity, and scary content.

03

Acts by parent rules

The extension can skip, mute, warn, or allow based on age profile and category settings.

Protection categories

Tune the content safety layer around your family.

Nudity

Helps identify scenes with exposed bodies or adult visual content.

Sexual Content

Flags intimate or sexually suggestive moments parents may want to avoid.

Violence

Detects fighting, weapons, injuries, and intense action sequences.

Language

Uses subtitle and audio-adjacent signals to help mute strong language.

Drugs / Alcohol / Tobacco

Recognizes substance-related scenes and recurring visual cues.

Scary Content

Helps avoid jump scares, frightening imagery, and high-intensity scenes.

Crude Humor

Marks rough jokes, gross-out moments, or content that needs context.

Dark Themes

Surfaces mature themes that may be better watched with an adult nearby.
